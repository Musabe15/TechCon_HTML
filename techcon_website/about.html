Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has since evolved into one of the most influential global technology conferences. Over the years, it has become a platform for innovation, learning, and collaboration across disciplines.

Our Mission

TechCon is driven by the goal of fostering technological growth and community. We believe in the power of shared knowledge, diversity of thought, and meaningful connections. Each year, we aim to inspire attendees with visionary speakers and hands-on opportunities.
